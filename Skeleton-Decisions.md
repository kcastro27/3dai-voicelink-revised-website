## Strategy (locked in — April 21, 2026)

- **Standalone site at `3daivoicelink.ai`** — domain available, not yet secured. Action item: Kevin to secure.
- **LeadTruffle is the skeleton** — section order, layout patterns, component types. We are NOT copy-pasting their code; we are using the same section scaffolding with our content.
- **Enrichments allowed section-by-section** — once the LeadTruffle-based skeleton is live, Kevin reviews each section and we pull in upgrades from Handshake, Rebolt, Quo, etc. where they improve a specific section.
- **Verbiage / copy is ours, not theirs** — we borrow structure, not language. All copy written in 3DAi VoiceLink brand voice.
- **"Company" tab = back-door** — looks like a normal About tab to a potential client. Internally, it's where we route to TrezDev parent-company info, employee portal, other product lines. Client-facing surface stays clean and single-purpose.
- **Perception play** — the site should make 3DAi VoiceLink look like a real, established, specialized AI voice-agent company, not a subsidiary side project. Parent-company credit is small footer-only.

## Section 1 — Hero ✅ LOCKED April 21, 2026

**Visual pattern:** Logo-forward, persona-forward, single clear CTA in the hero. Handshake-leaning (we borrow the persona playbook, write our own verbiage).

- **Logo at top of hero** — 3DAi VoiceLink logo, prominent, no eyebrow text above it
- **Grace as the hero's visual centerpiece** — transparent PNG, Handshake-style persona front-and-center
- **Hero CTA** — single primary CTA (exact wording TBD — "Book a Demo" or a Grace-specific variant)
- **Top-right nav persistent actions** — Log In + secondary CTA (wording must NOT echo the hero CTA so they don't compete)

**Layout to decide during mockup:** Centered vertical stack vs. Handshake-style split (Grace on one side, headline + CTA on the other). We'll try one in v1 and swap if it doesn't feel right.

**Copy guardrails:**
- Do NOT echo Handshake or LeadTruffle headlines
- No jargon (if a plumber can't parse it, rewrite)
- One clear value prop — not a paragraph

**Flag to check during build:** Is "Grace" in use by a direct competitor AI voice agent? Quick check before we commit the name to every section.
