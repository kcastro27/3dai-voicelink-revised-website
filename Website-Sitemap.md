## Reference-Site Cheat Sheet

| Site | Borrow | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Handshake** | Named AI persona (Julia → Grace), industry-specific demos, single-plan clarity, clean FAQ | Overly minimal footer |
| **LeadTruffle** | Testimonial carousel, integration logos, problem-framed use cases | Mega-menu complexity |
| **Smith.ai** | Industry segmentation | Gated homepage forms (too much friction) |
| **Rebolt** | Results-first hero, "One platform" framing, industry grid, mobile-app callout | Over-packed sections |
| **Quo** | Polished visuals, whitespace, video in hero, star ratings, "The Difference" positioning | Too many sections |
| **Current TrezDev site** | Live callback form, pricing transparency, ROI stats | Dev-editor aesthetic, TrezDev-only branding, no Grace, no testimonials, no "How It Works" |
